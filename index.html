About me

Hi, I'm Daniel. Though I often go by Dan. Welcome to my website.

My work primarily concerns backend systems engineering with a focus on relational databases. The vast majority of my professional experience involves working with C#, .NET (and its predecessor, .NET Framework), and Microsoft SQL Server. However I've also worked with alternative relational databases such as Postgres.

Once upon a time I even worked professionally with PHP and MySQL.

I currently work at Xero, a cloud-native FinTech SaaS company that provides accounting software and adjacent products.
